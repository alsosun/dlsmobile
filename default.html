Unit and Rubric Planner

Use this mobile companion to your Unit and Rubric Planner software to quickly browse curriculum documents. Choose your curriculum by openning the menu on the right. You can also view some of the many tutorials available.

If you have not used Unit and Rubric Planner, take a look around. Unit and Rubric planner is an easy to use unit, lesson and rubric planner that follows a backward design method that walks educators through curriculum outcomes, tiered planning, and differentiation of instruction and assessment. It even auto-generates rubrics!

 

PEDAGOGY

Unit and Rubric Planner is based on current pedagogical research, especially in the areas of backward design, inquiry, differentiation and assessment. Unit and Rubric Planner is easy to use and comes complete with built-in training videos for both the program and backward design curriculum planning.

BACKWARD DESIGN & DIFFERNTIATION

Starting with "Big Ideas" or essential questions, teachers can quickly browse and select learning outcomes and indicators. Activities are then planned directly from curricular outcomes. The program also provides tools and strategies for tiered activities and inquiry based learning.

Unit and Rubric Planner's built in differentiation strategies ensures planning for multi-level classrooms and all learning styles. Student questions and prompts are leveled by Bloom's Taxonomy, making tiered questioning and activity planning easier and more effective. Teachers can choose from dozens of instructional and assessment strategies, and they can research strategies right from within the program.

ASSESSMENT

Unit and Rubric Planner generates instant rubrics based directly on the selected learning outcomes or expectations. The rubrics are designed for "assessment as learning", not just for "assessment of learning".

Unit and Rubric Planner also has a powerful yet easy to use Student Success and Individual Education Plan creation tool. The program also includes tips and strategies for planning for all aspects of the learning environment.
